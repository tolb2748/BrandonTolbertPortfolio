Fatigue Analysis of a Cylinder Head Bolt on a Suzuki Motorcycle

Situation: Evaluate the structural integrity and fatigue life of the brake rotor bolt on a suzuki motorcycle

Task: Use concepts from mechanics of materials and verify the results with numerical simulation software.

Action: Applied fatigue models, FEA analysis, and rebuilt parts in Solidworks.

Result: Presented the results in front of academic and industry professional, receiving the top grade in the class on the project.
